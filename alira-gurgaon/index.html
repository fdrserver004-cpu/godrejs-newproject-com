Welcome To The Godrej Alira

Introducing The Godrej Alira, the latest premium residential offering by Godrej Alira is an exceptional residential elevation offering a unique blend of peaceful living and urban convenience in Central Gurugram. This exclusive development features 3 and 4 BHK apartments and presents only 120 premium residences. It is elegantly designed for spacious, private living. The project stands out for its layout which is thoughtfully designed to be wheelchair-friendly. It offers plush, Vastu-compliant, low-density living spaces for a heightened and secure living experience. The project presents 2 acres of lifestyle amenities including a palm-lined pool, a zen garden, an indoor gaming zones, a paddle court, badminton courts, a golf putting green, a jacuzzi, tree houses for kids, a stylish café & bar, and much more. Every amenity has been carefully curated at Godrej Alira Gurugram Sector 39 for all age groups.
