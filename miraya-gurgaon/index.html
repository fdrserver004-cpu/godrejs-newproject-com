About Godrej Miraya

Godrej New Launch Sector 43 Gurgaon is an ultra-luxury residential project located on Golf Course Road, offering spacious 3 and 4 BHK residences ranging from 2,800 to 4,000 sq. ft. Spread across 5.15 acres, the project features 3 high-rise towers with 248 exclusive units, ensuring privacy and luxury. Strategically positioned in Godrej Sector 43 Gurgaon, it offers seamless connectivity to NH 48, Rapid Metro, and IGI Airport. With world-class amenities such as an ultra-luxury clubhouse, a fitness center, and 24/7 security, Godrej New Launch promises an elevated lifestyle in the heart of Gurgaon.
